# Gap: where escalating absurdity in a negotiation-arc bit stops landing and turns into just weird or exhausting

- **Domain:** narrative-design / comedy
- **Type:** reaction
- **Status:** accumulating
- **Origin:** ideas entry `narrative-design / comedy / negotiation-arc / escalation` (one example of the technique, no documented sense of the right dose)

## Sessions

### 2026-07-10 · teacher
- **Scenario / mad-lib:** user in the middle of talking someone into something mundane, allowed to keep escalating how absurd the pitch gets — user chose "rename Taco Tuesdays to Tuna Tuesdays nationally"
- **User inputs:** the mundane goal (national Tuna Tuesday); steer supplied per artifact
- **Artifacts:** ~4 escalating pitch bits — mild slide deck; puns + fake official documents; special-forces handshake op; G7 treaty-style fist-bump
- **Steer / outcome:** puns / stacked meta-commentary / broadcast framing all failed; a single vivid tableau of an increasingly *serious* institution performing one literal absurd act kept landing and escalated cleanly (SEAL handshake → G7 fist-bump)
- **Mined:** "escalate WHO takes it seriously and how literally, not how the scene is packaged; one clean escalating image beats several stacked formats; puns are a different mechanism and don't inherit this technique" — provenance: Assumed (one session, one judge)
- **Left open:** one judge only — needs independent sessions/POVs before any of this is more than one person's taste; a follow-up round should test whether the "serious institution, literal act" rule holds outside the food-negotiation frame

### 2026-07-10 · teacher
- **Scenario / mad-lib:** Household-rule mad-lib: the user runs the household and institutes one mundane rule, then keeps escalating it. The session tested whether escalating the seriousness and literalness of WHO enforces the rule beats escalating how the bit is packaged — retesting the prior food-negotiation session's rule in a different frame.
- **User inputs:** The mundane rule: "everyone must announce themselves before entering the kitchen."
- **Artifacts:** Two escalation ladders. Ladder A escalated the enforcing institution (handwritten sign to laminated "Kitchen Entry Protocol" to a dinner-rush marshal to an HOA bylaw to a UN observer certifying compliance). Ladder B escalated the packaging (puns, a jingle, meta-commentary).
- **Steer / outcome:** Ladder A kept landing and climbed cleanly; Ladder B deflated — the same result as the prior food-negotiation session.
- **Mined:** The "serious institution performing one literal absurd act" rule replicates outside the food frame; packaging-based escalation stays a different, weaker mechanism. Provenance: Assumed — still one judge per session, and although two sessions now agree, their independence is not established, so this is a flag to look, not a convergence claim.
- **Left open:** Whether the rule holds for non-institutional escalation (escalating stakes rather than escalating enforcers); needs a genuinely independent judge before this is more than two aligned sessions.
